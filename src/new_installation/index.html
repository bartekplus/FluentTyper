Thank you for installing FluentTyper

FluentTyper attempts to improve the ease and speed of textual input by predicting words. It auto-completes words for you while you type, and can also fix your typos.

FluentTyper in action

Cheat sheet

  • After installing the extension, you must reload all open web pages or restart your browser to begin using it
  • As you type, FluentTyper will show a pop-up menu with a list of suggested words to complete your text
  • You can use the arrow keys (↑, ↓) to navigate the list of suggested words
  • Press Enter or Tab to confirm your selection
  • To hide the list of suggested words, you can either press the Escape key or continue typing
  • To undo autocorrection, press the Backspace key
